THE CORVETTE C4

EXPLORE HISTORY

THE FUTURE BEGINS

The Sharp Turn, Toward Modernity.

With the C4, Corvette entered the high-tech era. Everything was reengineered, from the chassis to the suspension to the digital dashboard. It introduced a more aerodynamic design and was the first Corvette to offer true modern handling characteristics. Tuned Port Injection brought power and response, while the ZR-1 “King of the Hill” variant made headlines with its Lotus-designed 32-valve V8. The C4 wasn’t just a visual overhaul, it was a performance reset for a new generation.

RACING ROOTS

The Corvette that Brought Precision into the Conversation.

The Corvette C4 made its mark in the automotive industry through endurance events and SCCA racing, where it showed off an improved package including suspension, responsive steering, and incredible chassis rigidity. The Z51 package gave weekend racers all the tools they needed, while the ZR-1 added elite straight-line speed. It may have looked lean and digital, but underneath was a car built to be pushed to its limits and driven to exhaustion.

HIGH-TECH, HIGH STYLE

Modernized Design, Competition Ready

Inside the C4, digital instrumentation and angular design reflected the cutting-edge feel of the 1980s and ’90s. With aircraft-inspired gauges and a wraparound dash, the cabin felt futuristic, because it was. Later models introduced better refinement, performance options, and improved interior ergonomics, marking the Corvette C4 as the first Corvette to truly straddle the trademarked luxury and performance of the brand without compromise.
