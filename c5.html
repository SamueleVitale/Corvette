THE CORVETTE C5

EXPLORE HISTORY

A NEW ERA BEGINS

A New Foundation for the Future.

The C5 wasn’t just a refresh, it was a full reimagining of what a Corvette could be. Built on an all-new chassis with a rear-mounted transaxle, it brought perfect 50/50 weight distribution and exceptional balance. The 5.7L LS1 V8 delivered responsive, reliable power, while the Z06 trim introduced serious track potential. Advanced for its time, the C5 featured drive-by-wire throttle, active handling, and improved aerodynamics, all while remaining a true driver's car.

LOUD & LEGENDARY

The Corvette that redefined the American sports car.

The Corvette C5 Z06 brought serious heat to the line-up, introducing a lighter hardtop design, stiffer suspension, and a naturally aspirated LS6 V8 pushing up to 405 horsepower. It wasn’t just fast in a straight line, it was nimble, communicative, and focused. Corvette’s engineers used racing experience to fine-tune the C5’s dynamics, giving it a firm yet balanced character that rewarded spirited drivers and track junkies alike.

THE ROAD AND BEYOND

Pure Power, Clean Design

Beyond its performance, the C5 delivered comfort and reliability in a way no previous Corvette had. With a refined ride, spacious hatch, and long-distance capability, it was just as comfortable on weekend getaways as it was hitting apexes. Inside, drivers enjoyed improved ergonomics, better materials, and modern conveniences, all wrapped in a body that looked just as sleek cruising highways as it did thundering down pit lanes.
