THE CORVETTE C6

EXPLORE HISTORY

THE SIXTH EVOLUTION

Sharper, Faster, and Built to Dominate.

The C6 Corvette was a performance leap, bringing more precision, power, and refinement than ever before. With the return of exposed headlights and tighter proportions, it looked ready to strike, and it delivered. The Z06, powered by a naturally aspirated 7.0L V8, stunned enthusiasts, while the ZR1's supercharged LS9 cranked out 638 horsepower. Lightweight materials, advanced suspension systems, and serious stopping power made the C6 not just fast, it made it a track predator that could rival Europe’s finest.

AGGRESSIVE WHERE IT COUNTS

Refined on the surface. Relentless underneath.

Whether in base trim or as a Z06, the C6 was engineered for serious drivers. The introduction of aluminum frames and carbon-fiber panels helped reduce weight, while race-derived brakes and suspension kept it composed under pressure. Acceleration was fierce, with 0–100 km/h times dipping below 4 seconds in high-performance trims. More than just an evolution, the C6 raised expectations and proved the Corvette wasn’t just keeping up, it was leading.

MADE WITH INTENTION

Track-Tuned.

While exterior changes made the C6 bolder and more aerodynamic, the interior saw a complete transformation. Chevrolet answered past criticism with better materials, improved fit and finish, and a more driver-focused layout. The cabin featured optional heads-up display, dual-zone climate control, and bolstered seating for confident cornering. The C6 finally brought refinement to match its raw performance, making it as enjoyable to daily drive as it was to dominate a track day.
